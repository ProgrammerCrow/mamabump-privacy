Privacy Policy for MamaBump

Last updated: December 12, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to MamaBump. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

MamaBump collects and stores the following information:

  • Personal Health Data: Pregnancy tracking data, period cycle information, weight records, and appointment notes that you voluntarily provide.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app usage data collected through Google AdMob for advertising purposes.
  • Local Storage: All your personal data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage.

3. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide pregnancy and period tracking functionality
  • Display your personal health records and appointments
  • Show personalized advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Improve app performance and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your personal health data is stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal information on our servers.
  • No Account Required: MamaBump does not require account creation or cloud synchronization.
  • Data Control: You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time through the app settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect device information, advertising ID, and usage data. For more information about how Google uses your data, please visit Google Privacy Policy.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal health information with third parties, except:

  • Advertising data shared with Google AdMob for ad display purposes
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Delete all your data at any time through app settings
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising through your device settings

8. Children's Privacy

MamaBump is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
